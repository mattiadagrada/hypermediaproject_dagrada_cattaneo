Transport

Description

Buy a ticket via SMS and pay it with your phone credit.

Activation and Rules

Open M-Bag and browse until you find the city you are interested in and click on buy ticket. You pay directly with you phone credit.

You will receive an SMS, the ticket is valid from that moment with no further validations required. You can find your ticket directly into your M-Bag, and you can renew it when it expires. If the ticket inspector asks for you ticket just show your SMS of confirm you have received.

In some cities you can also use the QRCode mode. You will receive a QRCode, along with the SMS, that will allow you to open the turnstiles. Put your device close to the reader and the deal is done.
