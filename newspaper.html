Activation and Rules

You can activate the offer online or in one of our physical store. The offer is activated on you rechargable phone card within 48 hours from the request e its activation is free and the first month is also free. You will receive an SMS with the confirm of the activation of the offer on your SIM. In order to use this service you will:

  1. Complete the activation with the link present in the SMS
  2. Define your credentials
  3. Download the application of the newspaper you chose and connect to the web site inserting your credentials

After the first free month, you will pay the cost of the subscription associated to the newspaper you have chosen. You can cancel the offer at any time, calling the Client Service 409.
